Discover Kendwa Rocks

Kendwa Rocks is a hidden gem that promises to take your breath away. This idyllic beach destination boasts of crystal clear turquoise waters, powdery white sand, and breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean. But there's more to Kendwa Rocks than just a stunning beach

Nestled on the shore of Kendwa beach is a place that is nothing short of magical - the Kendwa Rocks Restaurant. Imagine dining on freshly caught seafood with the ocean breeze caressing your skin and the sound of the waves lapping against the shore. This is the kind of experience that the Kendwa Rocks Restaurant offersbr

The menu at Kendwa Rocks Restaurant is a tribute to the flavors and spices of Zanzibar. The chefs take pride in using the freshest ingredients, locally sourced and prepared with the utmost care. From succulent grilled prawns to melt-in-your-mouth grilled octopus, every dish is a culinary masterpiece

What's more, the restaurant is situated in an exclusive location that can only be accessed by foot or by boat during high tide, adding a sense of adventure and exclusivity to your dining experience. You'll feel like you're on a secret mission to uncover the best-kept secret on the island

As the sun sets over the Indian Ocean, the sky transforms into a kaleidoscope of colors that will leave you mesmerized. The Kendwa Rocks Restaurant is the perfect place to soak in the beauty of Zanzibar, one delectable bite at a time

Whether you're a foodie looking for an exceptional dining experience or simply seeking to indulge in the natural beauty of Zanzibar, Kendwa Rocks Restaurant is the place to be. This is an experience that will leave you with lasting memories and a desire to come back for more
