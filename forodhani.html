Discover Forodhani Night Market

Forodhani Night Market is a vibrant and bustling food market located in Stone Town, Zanzibar. As the sun sets and the market comes alive, the air is filled with the enticing aromas of sizzling seafood, exotic spices, and freshly baked treats

The market is a food lover's paradise, with a huge variety of street food on offer. From savory grilled seafood and meat skewers to sweet treats like fried bananas and coconut candies, there is something to satisfy every taste bud. You can even try local delicacies like Zanzibari pizza or urojo, a tangy and spicy soup made with tropical fruits and spices

The atmosphere at Forodhani Night Market is electric, with colorful stalls and vendors vying for your attention and offering samples of their delicious wares. You can sit at one of the communal tables and mingle with locals and tourists alike, enjoying the lively energy of the market

All in all, Forodhani Night Market is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to experience the vibrant culture and cuisine of Zanzibar in a unique and unforgettable way.
